Relevant IG AR Filters

hallucinate

This IG Filter is one of the most satisfying filter that I realized. Both for my enjoyment of the artist and for the challenge that it gave me to create artistically and technically.

mood.txt

This IG Filter was born as an experiment to try different interactions and possibility to choose meshes, blended to the possibility of make a shot which has also the real-time timestamp in which the story was recorded.

This IG Filter was made after looking at a videoclip of the italian singer Ariete, the aesthetic of the video was one of my favourite ones: the old PCs' monitors. That sparked in me the idea to create a filter based on the old computers desktops.

fancyfootball

This IG Filter was the result of a workshop held by Meta Spark. It ended up as one of the most appreciated and featured also in the Facebook group and in the official IG page of Meta and the host of the workshop.
